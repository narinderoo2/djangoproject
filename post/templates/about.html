How to Write Meta Descriptions that Drive Traffic and Conversions
You’re working hard to market your company. You’re writing your product descriptions, optimizing your images, and mastering most of the other elements of effective search engine optimization (SEO). Still, if you’re like many business owners, one aspect of SEO could be giving you trouble: meta descriptions.
What is a meta description?
A meta description is meta tag that acts as a 155-160 characters summary that describes the content of a web page. Search engines show it in search results when the meta description also includes the keywords being searched. Reading other people’s meta descriptions will make the process look deceptively simple, but that simplicity is the very thing that makes them so hard to write.
